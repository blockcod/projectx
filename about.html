About

About Each year the Hudson Valley Festival presents the best in American and world folk music. Located in Chatham, NY, our festival celebrates down roots music and the traditions it embodies. The weekend-long event features a full bill of talent from New York’s own thriving folk music scene, as well as national and international blues, country and jazz artists. We invite the community to come listen, play and celebrate. We’ll have over 20 bands, instrument lessons and workshops, jam sessions, great food, and much more.

FAQ

Q: WHAT TIME DOES EVERYTHING OPEN?

Gates will open at 12pm on Friday, and 10am on Saturday and Sunday. Parking will open one-hour in advance of gates. You will need to have a valid festival wristband/ticket to enter the grounds.

Q: IS THERE PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION?

No. You must either rent a car, or coordinate a carpool

Q: CAN I BRING FOOD AND/OR DRINK?

Outside food or drink is not allowed. We have some great food trucks and craft vendors on-site, and public water is readily available. You can bring your own drink containers, provided that they are empty. Drink container will be searched at the gate.

Q: IS THERE CAMPING ON-SITE?

Sorry, no camping is available on-site, but there are campgrounds not far from the County Fair grounds. The Fox Hill Camping Area is only an 8-minute drive away, and a popular camping location.

Q: WHAT IS PROHIBITED ON THE FAIR GROUNDS?

  • Bicycles, mopeds, skateboards, rollerblades, heelies, or personal motorized vehicles
  • Coolers, glass containers of any kind, alcoholic beverages, illegal substances of any kind, firearms, noise-making devices, laser pointers, knives, box cutters, tools of any kind (including Leathermen) pepper spray, Mace, or any kind of item that could be used as a weapon.
  • Outside food and drink
  • Flag poles
  • Commercial audio visual equipment
  • Tents, umbrellas and shade structures over 30” are prohibited in lawn seating areas.
  • Pets
  • Drones

Q: WHAT IF THERE’S BAD WEATHER?

The festival runs rain or shine. If severe weather becomes an issue and we need to interrupt programming, announcements will be made regarding safety procedures. Programming will resume when deemed safe.

Q: IS THERE AN AGE RESTRICTION?

No -- all ages are welcome!

Q: HOW MUCH DOES PARKING COST?

Parking is free. Handicap parking is available.

Q: CAN I SMOKE?

Smoking is not allowed in any building, Grandstand or bleachers. Please find an isolated area if you must smoke, and dispose of your butts properly.

Q: CAN I DRINK ALCOHOL?

All guests over 21 will be provided a special wristband so you can purchase beer, wine, or cider. Please drink responsibly.

Q: WHAT HAPPENS IF I NEED FIRST-AID?

Minor injuries and ailments are treated at the First Aid Station located near the restrooms by the Main Entrance #1
