Information

The Hudson Valley Folk Festival is held at the Columbia County Fair in Chatham, New York which is a 40-minute drive southeast from Albany, New York and a 2-1/2 hour drive north from New York City. The Fairgrounds are located on Route 66/Hudson Ave.

FAQ

Q: WHAT TIME DOES EVERYTHING OPEN?

Gates will open at 12pm on Friday, and 10am on Saturday and Sunday. Parking will open one-hour in advance of gates. You will need to have a valid festival wristband/ticket to enter the grounds.
